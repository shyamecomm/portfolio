How I Think

This page will soon showcase interactive diagrams, sketches, and micro case studies that map my thought process from problem framing to shipped experience. For now, here is a quick snapshot of the principles I keep close while designing and building.

Systems before Screens

Document flows, constraints, and metrics first. Interfaces follow naturally when the system is coherent.

Momentum Mindset

Ship small proofs quickly, measure impact, then scale with confidence. Momentum beats perfection.

Human Signal

Every feature has a human at the other end. Empathy interviews and shadowing rituals stay in the loop.
